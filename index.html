Purpose

This system was created to help users discover titles of books depending on the genre they wish to read. The collection consists of various well known and liked books which are intended to be a good starting point for new readers. However if you feel like reading something more niche, our random book generator will give you suggestions.

Features

  • Filtering Books by Genre
  • Searching for books by title or author
  • Viewing synopses of books as well as interesting facts
  • Saving books to your wishlist
  • Contact page for any questions/suggestions
